One

Surrounded by a sea of people but alone,
Constantly feeling misunderstood,
Unable to get the words out your head and out through your lips,
The feeling of numbness flowing through you,
Trying hard to control everyone and everything you can,
Hoping that you’ll forget the one thing you badly need to control but can’t...

People say “It’s better to have loved and lost” but the truth is there is no greater pain than that of losing one you’ve loved.
